Azkaban · Urgent Ministry Dispatch

He Has Gone. Mass Murderer Sirius Black Vanishes From Azkaban in History's First-Ever Escape

Black, convicted in 1981 of the murder of twelve Muggles and the betrayal of the Potter family to He-Who-Must-Not-Be-Named, has apparently evaded an entire garrison of Dementors. Minister Fudge travels personally to inspect the fortress.
By Rita Skeeter, Senior Correspondent · Azkaban Fortress, North Sea

The impenetrable walls of Azkaban — long considered the most secure institution in the wizarding world — have been breached. Sirius Black, described by Ministry officials as "the most dangerous dark wizard alive short of You-Know-Who himself," was discovered missing from his cell during the early hours of Sunday morning. The Dementors, ordinarily infallible in their guardianship, appear wholly bewildered. Minister Cornelius Fudge, visibly shaken upon his arrival at the fortress, was heard to say only that the matter was being "looked into with the utmost urgency."
